Hi, I'm Una Chan — a PhD student at the University of Michigan.

My research sits at the intersection of **network systems** and **machine learning systems**. I'm interested in how ML can make networks more adaptive and efficient, and how systems design can make ML more reliable in practice.

Recent projects include:

- **AI for networks** — applying machine learning to network performance and management.
- **Medical AI** — building ML systems for healthcare applications.
